# Cybercrime Recovery & Reporting

## Description
Helps victims of online scams, UPI fraud, phishing, and financial cybercrimes report cases and recover funds.

## Trigger Keywords & Triggers
The skill triggers when the query contains words like: cybercrime, cyber fraud, online scam, upi fraud, money lost online, otp hack, scam, phishing, cyber cell, hacked.

### Positive Examples
  - "I lost Rs. 40,000 to an online job scam yesterday."
  - "Someone hacked my bank account via OTP."
  - "Where to report UPI cyber fraud?"

### Negative Examples
  - "How to divide ancestral property?"
  - "What is a Zero FIR?"

## Workflow Steps
1. Urge immediate reporting: Call National Cyber Crime Helpline 1930 within the 'golden hour' (first 2 hours).
2. Advise calling the bank immediately to freeze the beneficiary account / block cards.
3. Take screenshots of transaction SMS, UPI IDs, payment proofs, and chat records.
4. File a formal complaint online at cybercrime.gov.in or visit the local Cyber Crime Cell.
5. Collect the acknowledgment report and submit a chargeback dispute form at the home bank.

## Risk Detection Logic
Check for: Delay in reporting (decreases chance of freezing funds), sharing of sensitive credentials (may affect bank liability policy), and unofficial helpline scams.

## Output Format
Provide: Immediate checklist, 1930 helpline guidance, screenshot collection list, and draft dispute application to the bank.

## References Structure
- Information Technology Act, 2000 - Section 66D
- BNS, 2023 - Section 318 (Cheating)
- RBI Circular on Customer Liability in Unauthorized Electronic Transactions

- Bank Card Block application
